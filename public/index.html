RTB Compliance — What It Means

The Residential Tenancies Board (RTB) is Ireland's regulator for the rental sector. NestSeek helps protect both landlords and tenants:

  • For permanent rentals: Landlords must confirm RTB registration before listing. We block listings without it.
  • For temporary sublets: Short stays under 6 months are typically licence agreements, not tenancies, so RTB registration is not required.
  • Rent Pressure Zones: Most of Dublin has rent increase caps (2% per year). We inform landlords about this.
  • Tenant protection: You can verify a landlord's RTB number at rtb.ie before committing.

How NestSeek Works — Step by Step

A complete guide for tenants and property owners.

For Tenants (I Need a Place)

1
Create an account

Click "Login" → "Register" tab. Enter your name, email, phone, occupation, and password. You'll get a verification email.

2
Post what you need

Click "I Need a Place". Fill in:

  • Type: Temporary (1-6 months) or Permanent (6+ months)
  • Areas: Type each area and press Enter (e.g. "Dublin 2", "Rathmines")
  • Dates: When you need to move in/out
  • Budget: Your min-max range per month
  • Room type: Single room, studio, apartment, etc.
  • Preferences: Furnished, WiFi, parking, etc.
3
Wait for offers

Property owners browse your need and send offers. You'll see them in your Dashboard and get a notification. An email is also sent to you.

4
Accept or decline

In your Dashboard, each offer shows the owner's proposed rent and message. Click "Accept" to proceed or "Decline" to pass.

5
Get contact details

When you accept, both sides get each other's email and phone number via notifications and email. You can then arrange viewings, deposits, and move-in directly.

For Property Owners (I Have a Place)

1
Create an account

Same as above — register with your details.

2
Option A: Browse tenant needs

Click "I Have a Place" to see everyone looking for accommodation. Filter by type, area, room, and budget. Click "Make an Offer" on any need that matches your place.

3
Option B: List your property

Click "List Property" to post your place. The system automatically matches it with tenants who need your area, dates, and budget range — and notifies them.

For permanent rentals: You must confirm your RTB registration number before listing (Irish legal requirement).

4
Wait for acceptance

If a tenant accepts your offer, you'll get a notification and email with their contact details (email and phone). You can then arrange everything directly.

How Contact Exchange Works

NestSeek protects your privacy until you're ready:

Before accepting: Your email and phone are hidden. Only your name and occupation are visible.
↓
After accepting an offer: Both the tenant and owner receive each other's email address and phone number via:
  • In-app notification (check Notifications page)
  • Email to both parties
↓
From there: Arrange viewings, agree on terms, and move in — all directly between you.

RTB Registration (Required for Permanent Rentals)

Under Irish law, all private residential tenancies must be registered with the Residential Tenancies Board (RTB) within one month of commencement. You cannot list a permanent rental without RTB registration.
